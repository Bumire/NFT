More Than
a Restaurant

Sway Cuisine was born from a deep love of African food culture. the bold spices, the slow-cooked soups, the communal tables. We believe every dish tells a story of where it came from.

Located in the heart of Benin City, we bring together the finest local ingredients and techniques refined over decades. Every plate that leaves our kitchen carries the same pride and intention as the first one ever served.
