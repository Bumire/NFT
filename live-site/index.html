The Process

Six steps. Total transparency.
You're in control throughout.

📞
STEP 01
Free Consultation

We listen to your vision, budget, location, and timeline. Video call, WhatsApp, or email. No commitment, no charge.

🔍
STEP 02
Land Verification

We physically inspect the land, run government title checks, survey verification, and neighbourhood review — before any money moves.

📐
STEP 03
Architecture & Design

Our architects produce your plans. You review and approve remotely. Every detail confirmed in writing before construction begins.

🏗️
STEP 04
Construction Tracking

Weekly drone footage, photos, and live video calls. Your money releases in stages — foundation, structure, roofing, finishing — never in bulk.

🏠
STEP 05
Completion & Handover

Final inspection. Keys secured. Full handover report delivered. You choose when and how you take possession.

📦
STEP 06
Document Delivery

Every original — C of O, deed of assignment, approvals — packaged and shipped to your door via DHL or FedEx. Tracked and insured.

Security & Transparency

We didn't ask you to trust us.
We built systems that make it unnecessary.

The biggest risk when building from abroad isn't the construction — it's sending money to the wrong people. Every feature we built exists to prevent exactly that.

🔐 Escrow
Milestone Payment Protection

Your money never moves in a lump sum. It sits protected and only releases when each construction stage is physically verified by our team and signed off by you.

🚁 Drone
Weekly Drone & Video Reports

Unfiltered drone footage and ground-level video of your site every week. You see exactly what's happening. If there's a delay, you hear it from us first — not weeks later.

📹 Live
Live Construction Camera

Premium option: a live feed camera on site. Log in from London, Houston, or Toronto and watch your house being built in real time. This is what no other company in Nigeria offers.

📁 Vault
Digital Document Vault

Survey plan, land title, building approvals, contracts, receipts — all stored in your secure online vault. Accessible from anywhere in the world at any time.

🧾 Costs
Full Cost Transparency

Materials, labour, permits, finishing — itemised line by line. You receive receipts for every spend. No mystery markups. No hidden charges. You know where every naira goes.

⚖️ Legal
Binding Legal Contracts

Every project is backed by a signed agreement under Nigerian law. You receive a notarised original by DHL. Our in-house legal team is on record. Everything is enforceable.
